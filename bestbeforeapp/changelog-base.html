1.2.1

This update includes barcode scanner support and bug fixes.

What's New

  • Barcode scanner. Long press add button to scan.

This may not be the barcode scanner you are looking for, all barcodes should be added by yourselves, so it's only a quick way to renew/restock items.

I can only provide a barcode scanner at this level, because barcode on the back of your package, contains only a identifier of a kind of item. All we can get from it that's useful is the name, if we have a correct database for that. Sadly this kind of databases are always paid, not worthy when you only want the name.

We can't get production dates or any other dates from a barcode, so automatically instocking is just not possible, even if I pay for a database.

  • 3D Touch shortcut for barcode scan.
  • Allow setting alarm date between 1 day after best before date and right on best before date. In settings.
  • Allow regenerating all notifications, if you just can't recieve any (If the notify time is in your do not disturb range, you will not be notified) In settings.
  • Text views in forward strategy settings now supports multiple-line.
  • Delete item is renamed to archive. Yes they were not deleted at all, I will provide an archive view in future updates.
  • Allow production date later than today. Useful when you are renewing your domain name ahead of time.

Bug Fixes

  • Fix a bug that crashes the app in edit or renew view
  • Fix a bug that may crash the app when user searching for items, and receives synced updates at the same time.
  • Fix that sync is defaultly on for users that are not signed in into iCloud.
  • Fix that in-stock strategy for item kind not correctly generated.
  • Fix item detail texts.

Sorry for these bugs. If you find any other bug, please let me know by sending me email at bestbefore@intii.com. You can do that right from settings.
